# HomebaseED Logo Generation Brief

## Primary Prompt (for Imagen)

A clean, modern logo mark for "HomebaseED", an educational homeschooling platform.
The icon combines a simple geometric house silhouette with a subtle compass needle
integrated into the roofline, suggesting both "home" and "finding your direction."

Style: Flat vector, single color (deep forest green #1B6B4A) on transparent/white
background. Minimal line weight, no gradients, no shadows. The house shape should
feel architectural and confident, not cute or cartoonish. Think Zillow meets Khan Academy.

The icon should work at 32x32 favicon size and at full-width hero size. No text in
the icon itself (the wordmark is handled separately in code).

## Alternate Prompts

### Version B - Abstract compass-home
Minimal geometric logo icon: a compass rose where the north point forms the peak of
a house roof. Deep green (#1B6B4A) on white. Flat vector style, no gradients.
Professional and clean, suitable for an education technology platform. Single color.

### Version C - Lettermark hybrid
Modern logo mark: the letters "H" and "E" combined into a single geometric monogram
that subtly suggests a house shape. Deep forest green on white background. Flat design,
no textures or shadows. Professional, not playful. Works as a favicon.

## Technical Requirements
- Output: PNG with transparent background preferred
- Dimensions: 512x512 minimum
- Style: Flat vector / geometric
- Colors: #1B6B4A (deep green) on transparent
- Must be legible at small sizes (favicon, nav bar)
